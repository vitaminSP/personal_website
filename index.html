Brief Bio

I work at Google Zürich, doing research in the field of computer vision. I previously worked on improving the quality of Google's search engine. I did a postdoc before this, at INRIA Sophia Antipolis (France), working on novel techniques for bi-directional path tracing. I graduated my PhD under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Philipp Slusallek in Saarbrücken (Germany), working on real-time ray tracing and global illumination for commodity hardware.
